Tour 1

Amsterdam Forest Tour: Escape the city and take a peaceful ride through the beautiful Amsterdam Forest. Along the way, stop at the Forest Museum and the Amserdamse Waterleidingduinen for a picnic or a swim. Distance: 15 km. Estimated time: 3-4 hours.

Highlights:
  • Amsterdam Forest Museum
  • Amsterdamse Waterleidingduinen

Tour 2

Foodie Tour of Amsterdam West: Discover the vibrant food scene in Amsterdam West on this tasty bike tour. We'll visit a variety of local markets, food halls, and specialty food shops. Distance: 12 km. Estimated time: 3 hours.

Highlights:
  • Ten Kate Markt
  • Foodhallen
  • De Hallen

Tour 3

Historical Tour of Amsterdam East: Explore the rich history and culture of Amsterdam East on this bike tour. We'll visit landmarks such as the Oosterpark and the Tropenmuseum, and learn about the city's history and diversity. Distance: 10 km. Estimated time: 2-3 hours.

Highlights:
  • Oosterpark
  • Tropenmuseum
