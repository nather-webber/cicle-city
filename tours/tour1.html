Tour 1 - Discover Amsterdam's historic center

Our first tour will take you through Amsterdam's historic center, where you'll discover the city's rich history and culture. You'll see some of Amsterdam's most famous landmarks, such as the Dam Square and the Royal Palace, as well as lesser-known gems, like the Begijnhof. During the tour, you'll also ride through several picturesque neighborhoods, including the Jordaan and the Nine Streets.

Highlights:

  • Dam Square and the Royal Palace
  • Begijnhof
  • Jordaan neighborhood
  • Nine Streets

Points of Interest:

  • The Dam Square: The heart of Amsterdam, home to the Royal Palace, the National Monument, and the New Church.
  • Begijnhof: A hidden courtyard dating back to the Middle Ages, with a serene atmosphere and beautiful buildings.
  • Jordaan neighborhood: One of Amsterdam's most picturesque neighborhoods, known for its charming canals, narrow streets, and colorful houses.
  • Nine Streets: A charming shopping area with a variety of independent boutiques, vintage shops, and cozy cafes.

We recommend this tour for those who want to get a taste of Amsterdam's rich history and culture, and see some of the city's most famous landmarks. The tour takes around 2 hours, and we will stop several times along the way for photo opportunities and to take in the sights.
